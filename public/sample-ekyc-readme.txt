To demo Aadhaar Offline eKYC login:
1. Visit https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/offline-ekyc
2. Download your eKYC ZIP (requires Aadhaar + OTP once)
3. Upload the ZIP at the kiosk with your chosen Share Code

For judges: UIDAI provides sample XML at their developer portal at:
https://uidai.gov.in/en/915-developer-section/tutorial-section/11347-offline-ekyc-sample-data.html
